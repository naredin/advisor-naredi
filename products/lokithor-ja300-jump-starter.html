About This Product

Why the Lokhithor JA300 Jump Starter?

The Lokithor JA300 is a multi-tool emergency kit in one unit — jump starter, tyre compressor, power bank, and torch. Unlike supercapacitor jump starters that draw from the vehicle battery, the JA300 has its own lithium battery, so it starts instantly with no wait time. The 150PSI compressor inflates a flat car tyre from 0 to 35PSI in approximately 8 minutes. As a power bank it charges phones and devices via USB. For someone who wants one emergency tool that covers everything, the JA300 is the most practical option.

FAQ

Common questions

What is a supercapacitor jump starter and why is it better for India?
Traditional lithium jump starters use a battery inside that degrades over time, especially in heat. Supercapacitor jump starters use no internal battery — they draw energy from your car's own battery (even if flat) and amplify it enough to start the engine. They work in 55°C heat, last virtually forever, and are always ready even after sitting unused for a year.
My battery is completely dead — will a supercapacitor jump starter work?
Yes, but it needs a minimum voltage from your battery — typically above 2V. A battery that has been sitting completely dead for months may be below this. In that case, connect to another vehicle's battery for 5 minutes first, then use the jump starter.
How many times can I use a jump starter in one day?
Supercapacitor units (OzCharge) can be used repeatedly — each use recharges from the battery in 1-2 minutes. Lithium units (Lokithor JA300) can typically do 20-30 jumps on a single charge.
Can I use a jump starter to charge my phone or other devices?
The Lokithor JA300 has a USB power bank output — yes. OzCharge supercapacitor units do not have this feature as they have no internal stored energy.
