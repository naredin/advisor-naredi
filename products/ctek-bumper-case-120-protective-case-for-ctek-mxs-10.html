FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a Comfort Connect accessory or can I just use the clamps?
The clamps work perfectly for occasional charging. The Comfort Connect system is for people who charge regularly — it saves 3-4 minutes each time and eliminates any risk of reversing the polarity or accidental sparks on the battery terminals.
What is the LED indicator on the Comfort Indicator accessories?
A 3-LED display: green solid means battery is fully charged and healthy, green flashing means charging in progress, amber means battery is below 75% and should be charged, red means battery is critically low and needs immediate attention.
Can I use CTEK accessories with non-CTEK chargers?
CTEK Comfort Connect accessories use a proprietary connector designed for CTEK chargers. They cannot be used with other brands' chargers. The connector is a deliberate design — it is polarised (cannot be connected incorrectly) and rated for the CTEK current output.
