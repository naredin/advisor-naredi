About This Product

Why the Rislone High Mileage Engine?

For vehicles over 80,000km showing symptoms of wear — reduced power, slightly rough idle, increased oil consumption, slightly elevated fuel consumption — the High Mileage Engine Repair is a comprehensive treatment. It addresses multiple aspects of engine wear simultaneously: friction reduction, seal conditioning, and oil film enhancement at worn surfaces.

How It Works

The 4-step process explained

1
Friction Modifiers
Reduces friction at worn surfaces — particularly pistons, rings, and valve train components.
2
Oil Film Enhancement
Thickens the oil film at worn surfaces where the oil clearance has increased with wear.
3
Seal Conditioning
Rejuvenates aged rubber seals.
4
Compression Improvement
Improved piston ring lubrication can partially restore compression in mildly worn engines.

Are Rislone additives safe for Indian vehicles?
Yes. Rislone is an American brand formulating automotive additives since 1921. Their products are designed to be compatible with all modern engine oils, coolants, and transmission fluids. They are sold in over 70 countries and are safe for Indian petrol and diesel engines.
Can I use a stop leak additive as a permanent fix?
Stop leak additives condition rubber seals to swell slightly and re-seat. They work on slow seeps and minor leaks and the effect can last 30,000-50,000km. They are not a replacement for a mechanical repair on a major leak — but for minor seeps, they are a legitimate and commonly used solution.
Will fuel additives void my car's warranty?
No — adding a fuel system cleaner to the tank is no different from using a different petrol pump. Indian car manufacturers do not prohibit fuel additives. If a dealer ever claims this, ask for the specific clause in the warranty document.
How often should I use a fuel system cleaner?
Once every 10,000-15,000km or approximately once a year for normal use. If you use a specific station known for poor quality fuel, every 5,000km is reasonable. The cost is ₹900-2,400 and it takes 30 seconds to add to the tank.
